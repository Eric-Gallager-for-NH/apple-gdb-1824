The Heisenberg Debugging Technology

Class #509, Embedded Systems Conference West, September 1999

A lecture / presentation on the Introspect feature of GDB, which allows users to record and play back debugging sessions on a remote embedded system, with far less impact on timing and synchronization that is seen in ordinary breakpoint debugging.
